About KGMOA

Kerala Government Medical Officers Association (KGMOA) represents government doctors statewide. We collaborate with stakeholders to strengthen primary and secondary care, drive health system improvements, and support professional growth.

  • • Advocacy for robust public health infrastructure
  • • Continuous professional development & CME
  • • Ethical, patient-centric leadership
  • • Research, innovation & quality
